Suzanne's Work

Suzanne Collin’s work is a gripping blend of heart-pounding action, immersive storytelling, and unforgettable characters that stay with you long after the final page.

If you’re looking for stories that captivate your imagination and leave you wanting more, Suzanne Collin’s books are a must read.

Books

The Hunger Games

In the ashes of a fallen North America, the Capitol keeps control through a brutal televised competition known as the Hunger Games. Each district must sacrifice one boy and one girl to fight to the death, and when sixteen-year-old Katniss Everdeen volunteers to save her sister, she becomes a symbol of defiance she never intended to be. This haunting audiobook captures every heartbeat of fear, courage, and rebellion in vivid, cinematic detail.

Catching Fire

After surviving the Hunger Games, Katniss and Peeta return home as victors—but their defiance has sparked something dangerous. The Capitol wants revenge, and rebellion is spreading through the districts. As Katniss is thrust back into the arena, she must face impossible choices about love, loyalty, and survival. The second audiobook intensifies the suspense, blending political intrigue, action, and raw human emotion in Suzanne Collins’s signature style.

Mockingjay

The revolution has begun, and the world burns around Katniss Everdeen. Haunted by the past and torn between duty and heart, she becomes the reluctant face of a movement that could either free Panem from it's past or destroy it entirely. As alliances fracture and the truth of the games unfolds, this powerful final book delivers a breathtaking and devastating conclusion to the series, exploring sacrifice, justice, and the fragile cost of hope.
